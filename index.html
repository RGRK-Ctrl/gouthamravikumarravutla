About

Product built on evidence, shipped with conviction.

I'm a product manager focused on operationalizing AI and data for enterprise customers. My work sits where messy real-world workflows meet emerging technology — the place where a demo is easy but a durable, adopted product is hard.

I lead discovery to separate the problem worth solving from the noise, frame the opportunity so engineering and GTM pull in the same direction, and ship in slices that de-risk the roadmap. I'm comfortable owning the full arc: user research, positioning, prioritization, launch, and the unglamorous adoption work that decides whether a product survives.

Recent focus: GenAI product suites — document intelligence, retrieval, conversational analytics — and the governance and change-management that enterprise buyers demand before they trust them.

Document intelligence for a research publisher
